Victorian Christmas Market 2023

Date: 02 December 2023 - 03 December 2023

Gather under the gas lamps and celebrate the start of the festive season as the much-loved Victorian Christmas Market returns to Sheffield's Kelham Island Museum for a weekend of traditional cheer 2 & 3 December.


Gifts & Local Markers

Over 80 market stalls selling an array of Sheffield made wares, quality gifts and seasonal goods, you'll find the perfect presents and stocking fillers for your loved ones.


Eat, Drink & Be Merry

Enjoy food and drink, from traditional hog roast to wood fired pizza. Relax in the Millowners Arms or warm up with a mulled wine. Enjoy the thrill & excitement of the Family Fairground with rides, games and fairground favourites to feast on.


Join in the Festive Cheer, Singing Loud for all to Hear

Magical music and merriment from local brass bands, musicians, choirs and carollers on the Music Hall Stage. Don't miss the wonderful walkabout characters, traditional dancers and the amazing Snow Queen on stilts.


Family Fun & Santa comes to Sheffieldr

You'll be spoilt for choice with Christmassy crafts in Santa's Workshop, miniature engine rides on the Kelham Flyer, the return of the Selfie Sleigh plus meet the main man himself in Santa's Grotto.

Marvel at the Museum

Explore the galleries, learn about Sheffield's steel story and feel the floor shake as the mighty River Don Engine runs on the hour, every hour!

Prices : Adult (£8)

Children under 16 FREE

Firth Park Christmas Makers Market

Date: 09 December 2023 10.00am- 16.00pm

Local makers, bakers and growers will be taking part in Firth Park's first Community Makers' Market on Saturday 9 December.

Come along and buy handmade gifts from a range of local makers, enjoy a mince pie or mulled wine, meet up with friends and family, or go see Santa's Christmas Lights Switch On by Gill Furniss MP from 3pm. There will also be local brass bands and choirs providing Christmas cheer.

Winter Wonderland - Christmas in Nottingham

Tuesday 7 November - Sunday 31 December 2023 Old Market Square

Winter Wonderland returns to Nottingham and is extending the magic this Christmas!


Nottingham’s premier Christmas entertainment attraction will once again include the Ice Rink and Sky Skate ice path, plus the popular Ice Bar, Sur La Piste and Altitude ski bars.


Open for an extra week this year, Winter Wonderland runs from November until New Year's Eve. Festive fun seekers can choose to skate around Old Market Square, metres above the crowd, quench their thirst with a festive drink at one of two skate-up bars, or for those not so keen on heights, enjoy the ground-level ice rink.


For anyone who doesn’t feel like putting their skates on, there is a themed Ice Bar, kept at a chilling -10 degrees. Keep warm with the gloves and coats provided and enjoy a drink from an ice glass. This experience is not to be missed, so book early to avoid disappointment. .

Ultimate 90s 2024- Part of The Darley Park Weekender

31 August 2024

Take a fond trip back to the 90s where Buffy was on everyone’s top watch list and Alicia Silverstone was a style in icon in Clueless. Taking us back to this pop-tastic time are some of the greatest pop legends whose posters adorned the wall of many teenagers growing up.


Brit Award-winning boyband Blue, consisting of members Simon Webbe, Duncan James, Antony Costa and Lee Ryan, have sold 15 million records worldwide. All Rise formultiple hits including Too Close, If You Come Back and Sorry Seems to Be the Hardest Word.


R&B girl group Honeyz had five UK top 10 hits Finally Found, End of the Line, Love of a Lifetime, Never Let You Down and Won't Take It Lying Down. Original members Celena Cherry and Mariama Goodman will take to the stage to bang out the hits.


DJ Chris Moyles is best known for The Chris Moyles Show, on BBC Radio 1 from 2004 and more recently on Radio X. The show came live from Derby’s Guildhall Theatre in 2010, with guests McFly! He’ll take the party up a notch as he takes to the decks with his 90s Hangover DJ set.  


The Ultimate Boy Band Party Show is 5 bad boys with the power to rock you, taking you back to the naughty 90s withover 30 number one hits. With more key-changes than a Casio PT-50 the boys will be body poppin’ to bangers from Take That, Nsync and The Backstreet Boys as well as stepping from their stools for favourites from Westlife & many more… cue the wind machine!


Local Derby band Cassette Roulette are a 5-piece band made up of full-time professional musicians and two of the best female singers around. They’ve entertained crowds at hundreds of events across the UK, playing the perfect mix of old favourites and modern classics to keep the dance-floor full, all killer no filler!

Northern Lights

26 Dec 2023 - 9 Jan 2024

Join us for an immersive Northern Lights experience like no other in the QUAD Gallery between 9th December and 9th January. Based on the Aurora Borealis (‘Northern Lights’), this not-to-be-missed experience is created through lighting, projection, and sound to transport you across the globe for an ethereal experience.


Take Instagram worthy snaps in the space or sit and take it all in while enjoying a peaceful moment amidst the magic.


To elevate your experience, we will be serving festive food & drinks, including hot chocolate, from QUAD Café to enjoy and keep you in the festive spirit.
